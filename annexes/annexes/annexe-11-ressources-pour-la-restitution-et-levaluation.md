# Annex 11 - Resources for Reporting and Evaluation

The reporting of the Bilan Carbone® requires various supporting documents listed in [section 6](https://app.gitbook.com/s/GBSULMB7RDjF3KmSrnc9/6-synthese-et-restitution). Among these, supporting documents necessary for the evaluation of assessments are required, as described in [section 7](https://app.gitbook.com/s/GBSULMB7RDjF3KmSrnc9/7-evaluation-et-qualite-du-bilan-carbone-r).

Below are the supporting documents to be completed by the organisation before presenting its assessment.

## Management Commitment Letter

A blank free template is available to [duplicate or download here](https://www.canva.com/design/DAGZug8L_Ko/NqeDqjRXuGABMeewc-5t2w/edit?utm_content=DA\[%E2%80%A6]m_campaign=designshare\&utm_medium=link2\&utm_source=sharebutton).

## Blank Assessment Evaluation Report

This is accessible in annex 1 of the methodological guide for the evaluation of Bilan Carbone® assessments ([bibliography](https://www.bilancarbone-methode.com/annexes/bibliographie#guide-devaluation-des-bilans-ges)).
