## Transition Action Valuation (TAV)

The impact of [actions](../../5-plan-de-transition/5.2-construction-du-plan-daction.md) is not always quantifiable. It is then important to rely on a method for qualifying impact. The Transition Action Valuation (TAV) aims to determine and valorise relevant actions to help actors in their transition. This contribution is assessed via several criteria but more specifically by: relevance, contribution, adequacy.

## Steering Emission Reductions

Carbon accounting is also useful for steering emission reductions. The Greenhouse Gas Management System is a tool developed by the ABC. It makes it possible to manage GHGs in the same way as energy. It ranges from the documentation of GHG assessments to the monitoring of actions and renewal of associated commitments.
